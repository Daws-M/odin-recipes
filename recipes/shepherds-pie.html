Shepherd's Pie

Description

One of my personal favorites is shepherd's pie. Consists of ground beef stew, topped with cheesy mashed potato. Is hands down one of the best flavors that you could imagine.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef or lamb if you prefer
  • Mashed potatos
  • Vegetables
  • Flour
  • Ketchup
  • Beef broth
  • Cheese

Directions

  1. Boil potatos until tender, but still slightly firm. Mash the potatoes and mix in the butter, cheese, and onion. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  2. Boil the carrots until tender, but still slightly firm. Mash the carrots.
  3. Cook the onions and ground beef until the onions are clear and the beef is browned. Drain the fat, then add flour, ketchup, and beef broth. Simmer the mixture for 5 minutes.
  4. In a casserole dish, add the ground beef mixture. Next add the carrots, then teh mashed potatoes. Finally, top the casserole with cheese and bake until golden brown.

This recipe is a sure fire way to keep your family happy at dinner time.

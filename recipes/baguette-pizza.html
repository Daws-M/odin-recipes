Baguette Pizza

Amazing Baguette Pizza

If you ever get the craving for a pizza, but you want a cheaper faster version. These pizza baguettes are the thing for you. All it takes is 25 minutes and you have yourself a delicious homemade pizza.

Ingredients

  • 1 French baguette
  • ½ cup pizza sauce, or as needed
  • ⅔ cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 ounces cooked and crumbled sausage
  • 2 ounces mini pepperoni slices

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. Split baguette in half lengthwise. Warm pizza sauce in a microwave-safe bowl until hot about 45 seconds.
  3. Spread pizza sauce on baguette, sprinkle on cheese, and scatter sausage and pepperoni on top as desired. Place onto a baking tray.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until cheese is golden, about 18 minutes. Optional step: turn on the oven's broiler, set a rack 6 inches below the heating element, and broil the pizza for a deeper color, 1 to 2 minutes.
  5. Cut pizza into slices. Serve and enjoy.

This recipe is very easy to make, and is a great alternative to pizza.
